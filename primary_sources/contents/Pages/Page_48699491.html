1 Introduction

This document is written for programmers working on Wii U applications. It contains a number of precautions to be aware of during development.

You may use either method below to navigate the precautions and check whether any apply to your application.

  • By Keyword
    • Allows you to check precautions by related keywords.
  • By Page
    • Allows you to check precautions by the volume (general category) they are sorted into.
